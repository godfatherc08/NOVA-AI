Parameter Information
camera_angle: Vertical angle of camera relative to subject (eye_level, low_angle, high_angle, dutch_angle, overhead, birds_eye)

lens_mm: Focal length in millimeters (8-200mm). Lower = wider view, Higher = compressed perspective

shot_size: How much of subject is in frame (extreme_wide, wide, medium, close_up, extreme_close_up)

camera_height_ft: Physical height of camera from ground in feet (0-20ft)

lighting_type: Overall lighting style (natural, low_key, high_key, three_point, rembrandt, silhouette, chiaroscuro)

lighting_direction: Primary light source direction (front, side, back, top, bottom, rim)

color_temperature: Warmth/coolness of light in Kelvin (2000-10000K). Lower = warmer, Higher = cooler

time_of_day: Natural lighting period (dawn, morning, midday, golden_hour, dusk, night, blue_hour)

depth_of_field: Amount of background blur (shallow, medium, deep)

aperture: F-stop value controlling depth of field (f/1.4 - f/22). Lower = more blur

focus_distance: Distance to focus point in feet

color_grade: Overall color treatment (natural, warm, cool, desaturated, noir, sepia, teal_orange, vintage)

saturation: Color intensity (0.0-2.0). 1.0 = normal, <1.0 = muted, >1.0 = vibrant

contrast: Difference between lights and darks (0.0-2.0). 1.0 = normal

brightness: Overall exposure level (-2.0 to +2.0). 0 = normal

camera_movement: Type of camera motion (static, pan, tilt, dolly, track, crane, handheld, steadicam)

movement_speed: Speed of camera movement (slow, medium, fast, whip)

composition_rule: Framing guideline (thirds, golden_ratio, center, symmetry, diagonal)

subject_position: Where subject is placed (center, left, right, top, bottom, corner)

aspect_ratio: Frame dimensions (16:9, 2.39:1, 4:3, 1:1, 9:16)

film_stock: Film emulation type (digital, 35mm, 16mm, 8mm, super8, imax)

grain_intensity: Amount of film grain (0.0-1.0). 0 = none, 1 = heavy

vignette: Edge darkening intensity (0.0-1.0)

lens_distortion: Barrel/pincushion effect (-1.0 to +1.0)

chromatic_aberration: Color fringing on edges (0.0-1.0)

weather: Environmental conditions (clear, overcast, rain, snow, fog, storm)

atmosphere: Overall mood (tense, peaceful, mysterious, dramatic, eerie, romantic)

fog_density: Amount of fog/haze (0.0-1.0)

shadow_intensity: Darkness of shadows (0.0-1.0)

highlight_intensity: Brightness of highlights (0.0-1.0)
